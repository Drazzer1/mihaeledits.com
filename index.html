About Me

I craft thumb-stopping edits that hook in the first 3 seconds, keep watch-time high, and convert views into followers and customers.

I’m Mihael—editor, motion nerd, and pacing addict. I turn long-form into snappy, on-brand shorts using dynamic framing, rhythm-matched cuts, clean captions, and subtle SFX that sell the moment without shouting.
